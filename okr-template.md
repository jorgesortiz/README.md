## What are OKRs?

- **Objective** — What you want to achieve. Qualitative, inspiring, memorable.
- **Key Result** — How you measure progress. Quantitative, time-bound, binary (done or not).
- **Initiative** — What you will actually do to hit the key result.

> Rule of thumb: 1–3 objectives per quarter. 2–4 key results per objective.
