## What is SpendingHabits?

SpendingHabits is a personal finance platform that helps users visualize 
their spending habits and receive personalized insights to improve their 
financial health.

The platform syncs with bank accounts and credit cards to automatically 
track transactions, surfacing timely alerts on areas that need attention — 
bill changes, overspending, and budget drift — without the judgment or 
complexity of traditional budgeting tools.

---

## The problem it solves

Most people know they should budget. Very few actually do — not because 
they lack discipline, but because existing tools are complex, intimidating, 
and built for finance enthusiasts, not everyday users.

SpendingHabits meets users where they are: simple, friendly, and 
actionable. Less spreadsheet, more lifestyle.
